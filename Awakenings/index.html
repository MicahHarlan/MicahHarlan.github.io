My Data Science Portfolio

Surf Forecasting In Python

This project is an ongoing personal research project that pulls data from various sources online. These sources include different buoys off the coast of Virginia Beach, National Weather Service APIs, and Surfline's APIs. The end goal of this project is: using the data I've collected from the mentioned sources, it will provide me with an application like Surfline that tells me the conditions of the waves.

What is Surfline

According to Wikipedia Surfline is a company based out of Huntington Beach California that provides surf forecasting for surfers all over the world. Making it easier for surfers to check the waves.

Inspiration

Each year during my undergraduate studies and before, I would always look forward to surfing during the summer. I would activate my monthly subscription to Surfline which is expensive. However before the summer of 2023, I realized I could combine my studies and my lifelong knowledge of surfing and make my own surf forecasting application for the surf spots I go to.
