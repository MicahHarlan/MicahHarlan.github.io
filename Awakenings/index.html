Welcome to Awakenings

Thank you for considering Awakenings Counseling Center. Our clinicians and staff are committed to providing the highest quality of service to meet the counseling needs of children, adolescent, adults and families.

Every Individual has a unique journey

From time to time everyone faces stress or major life transitions. We recognize that each individual has a unique journey and maybe presented with difficult challenges. We encourage each person to use life’s difficulties as an opportunity for growth and healing mentally, emotionally, physically and spiritually.

Through our expert professional staff and with the use of many creative approaches, we are able to assist with a wide variety of problems facing individuals, couples and families today. Please take this opportunity to explore our website.

Our professional staff has vast experience treating many different issues such as, anxiety, PTSD, Depression, Alcohol and Drug Addiction, Relationship and Sex Addiction, Codependency, Women’s Issues, Sex Abuse, Behavior Problems in Children, Transition Issues, Anger Management, Couple’s Communication, Issues related to Chronic Illness, ACOA Issues, Grief and Loss, Gender Identity Issues, Eating Disorders, Divorce and Parenting Issues, stress, trauma, Attention Deficit Disorder, Traumatic Loss, and Compassion Fatigue just to name a few.

Our Professional Staff has extensive experience meeting the needs of our Military Members and their families. We are conveniently located near several Military Bases, which provides easy access for Service members.
